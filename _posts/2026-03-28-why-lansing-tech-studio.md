When we were choosing a name, we didn’t want something that sounded like a class… or a program… or just another after-school activity.

We chose Lansing Tech Studio on purpose.

A studio is where creators create.
It’s where ideas are tested.
Where prototypes are built.
Where innovation happens.

At Lansing Tech Studio, students aren’t just learning about technology — they’re using it to build, design, problem-solve, and lead.
They are:
* The creators
* The next-gen leaders
* The innovators

Everything we do is centered around giving students space to think boldly, try bravely, fail safely, and build confidently.

A studio isn’t about consuming.
It’s about creating.

And that’s exactly who our students are becoming.
