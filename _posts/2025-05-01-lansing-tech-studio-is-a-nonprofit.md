We are absolutely thrilled to share some incredible news: **Lansing Tech Studio is now an official 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization!** This is a monumental step forward for our community and our mission.

What started as a simple backyard LEGO club has blossomed into a vibrant, year-round tech hub for the youth of Lansing. Building on the foundational success and spirit of the Lansing Homeschool Hackers, we've grown into a dynamic space bustling with cutting-edge technology, dedicated mentorship, and hands-on programs spanning robotics, coding, design, media, and leadership.

### Our Mission

Our core mission remains unwavering:
**To empower the next generation by igniting their passion for technology, innovation, leadership, and service, cultivating tomorrow’s leaders driven to shape a better future in their communities and the world.**

### What We’re Building

With this new nonprofit status, we're not just running programs; we're transforming our FLL robotics initiatives into a full-blown student tech community. Our ambitious goal is to raise up confident, skilled, and principled leaders who are ready to shape the future of Lansing—and indeed, the world. We are achieving this through:

*   **FIRST LEGO League (FLL) Robotics Teams:** Fostering competitive spirit, problem-solving, and teamwork.
*   **Tech Workshops:** Hands-on learning experiences in diverse tech fields.
*   **Youth Hack-a-thons:** Challenges that inspire creative solutions and collaboration.
*   **Service Projects:** Using tech skills for community impact.
*   **Youth Leadership Tracks:** Developing the leadership qualities essential for tomorrow’s innovators.

Becoming a 501(c)(3) nonprofit means we can significantly expand our impact, forge stronger partnerships, and launch new, innovative initiatives that were previously out of reach. But we cannot do this alone.

### Join Us in Building the Future

This exciting new chapter requires collective effort, and we are actively seeking support from individuals and organizations who believe in the potential of our youth.

**We’re looking for:**

*   **Funding:** Financial contributions to expand our programs, acquire new equipment, and offer scholarships to ensure accessibility for all.
*   **Volunteers:** Passionate individuals to serve as mentors, coaches, workshop assistants, and event coordinators.
*   **Community & Corporate Partners:** Organizations eager to collaborate on projects, provide resources, or offer valuable industry insights and opportunities.

Your involvement, no matter how big or small, will make a tangible difference in the lives of our students and the future of our community.

**Ready to learn more or get involved?** [Contact us today](/contact)! Let’s build something truly amazing—for our kids, our city, and the future.
